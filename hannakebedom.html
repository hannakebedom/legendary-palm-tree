Commerce + Computer Science

The application of computer science to commerce can be powerful. When studying commerce at the University of Ottawa, I fell in love with financial technology. To think that software systems can be used to provide financial services amazes me. In the future, using my love for both fields, I hope to work to create applications that revolutionize how financial services are delivered. Shopify is an excellent example of how technology can revolutionize commerce. Through Shopify, thousands of merchants have the opportunity to sell their products online to a diverse audience.
